What you need to know:

Entry

You must set a PIN before you can access the space. Click the generate PIN on your profile page and set your desired PIN code. Keep it safe.

To enter the main door, first enter the VHS suite number: 104. This is also written on the directory beside the keypad.
There will be a prompt to enter your pin. Your pin is {{pin}} and can be changed in your profile page. The door will click unlocked, and you can enter.
The code for the inner door is now 1234. Enter this code, then turn the thumbturn toward the hinges.

All together: 104, then {{pin}}, then 1234 inside.

Extra tips:

  • Type 0104 to make the buzzer dial faster
  • You don't need to wait for the voice prompt to stop talking before entering your PIN
  • Leading zeroes in your PIN are optional
  • Dial * after your pin to skip the timeout and buzz-in faster

Exit

CLOSE THE WINDOWS, make sure the space is empty, turn the lights off including the isvhsopen.com switch, and exit.
To lock the inner door after you have left, press the Schlage button on the top, then rotate the thumbturn away from the hinges.

Please test the door to confirmed that it is closed and locked properly. 

For more info on opening and locking the space, see the wiki page.
