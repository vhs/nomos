What you need to know:

Entry

To enter the main door, first enter the VHS suite number: 104. This is also written on the directory beside the keypad.
There will be a prompt to enter your pin. You don't have to wait for the prompt to finish talking. Your pin can be changed by you, and is written in your profile page. The door will click unlocked, and you can enter.
The code for the inner door has not changed. Enter this code, then turn the thumbturn toward the hinges.

All together: 104, then YOUR PIN, then the old VHS pin for inside.

Exit

CLOSE THE WINDOWS, make sure the space is empty, turn the lights off including the isvhsopen.com switch, and exit.
To lock the inner door after you have left, press the Schlage button on the top, then rotate the thumbturn away from the hinges.

Please test the door to confirmed that it is closed and locked properly. 

For more info on opening and locking the space, see the wiki page.
